The Problem We Exist to Address

A misallocation of attention and talent

There is a massive misallocation of attention and talent in our culture. The people who might drive a real revival of serious thought in our timeâ€”essayists, historians, philosophers, poets, cross-disciplinary mindsâ€”are scattered, isolated, and underdeveloped. The institutions that should be finding and forming them mostly aren't.

Universities still have real strengths, but many of their best students and junior scholars experience them as more about bureaucracy, politics, and metrics than about understanding. Outside universities, would-be thinkers try to assemble an intellectual life from Twitter, Substack, podcasts, and Discord.

Historically, when a civilization changes its mind about itself, it happens because scenes form“dense, cross-cutting networks of people who argue, read, fall out, and slowly produce new common sense.

The Avalon Institute

The French Enlightenment had its salons. The 19th-century Germans had Jena and Berlin. If existing universities cannot play that role now, we need an institution that will.

Our San Francisco Venue

Avalon House

Our house in Twin Peaks has become a continuous, living salon. Since September 2024 we have hosted poetry readings, string quartets, performances of Platonic dialogues, evenings devoted to Nietzsche and Hildegard von Bingen, salons that move freely between genetics and Augustine, AI alignment and Euripides. Food, wine, a piano; people sitting on the floor arguing about texts at midnight.

For a certain slice of the culture, Avalon is already where you go when you are in San Francisco and want to know what the live currents of thought actually are this year.

Programs & Initiatives

Avalon Residency

A residency at Avalon House for exceptional young humanists and independent scholars operating above the ambient level of existing institutions.

Field Building

We create events and connections suited to a culture's self-development, serving as a convening hub for aligned organisations.

The Avalon Fellowship

An inaugural cohort programme providing institutional resources, mentorship, and peer environment for those who have yet to find their place in the wider world.
